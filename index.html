Burlygames is an Independent Game and Tool Development Company located in Portland Oregon, USA.

Founded in 2016 as a solo game development effort, Burlygame's current mission is to provide tools and assets to see how far a solo or small team of developers can push procedural content generation for use in their own game development.   

Leveraging the power of SideFX Houdini, digital asset tools are being developed for use with the Unreal Engine 4 and Unity game engines. These tools will be sold through the Unreal and Unity marketplaces, as well as on Gumroad and made available to our Patreon supporters.
